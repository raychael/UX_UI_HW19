Some Stuff About Me

Hello, my name is Rachel Bradley and I am a UX/UI designer. I have a Bachelor’s degree in Business Economics and a UX/UI design certificate, both from the University of Arizona.

I love the challenge of creating a site that makes for an easy and effective user experience while representing the site in a way the company is proud of.

MY SKILLS

User Research

My Skills include conducting user and stakeholder interviews, creating proto-personas, empathy maps, affinity diagrams, competitor analysis’, and heuristic evaluations.

Definition and Ideation

My Skills include creating problem statements, UX hypotheses, value proposition canvases, feature prioritization matrix, card sorting, sitemapping, user flow diagrams, user journey maps, conducting the I like, I wish, what if method, user scenarios, and storyboards.

Wireframing and Prototyping

My Skills include paper, low-fidelity, and high-fidelity prototyping and low-fidelity and high-fidelity wire framing.

Usability Testing

My Skills include conducting usability testing and iterating based on results.
